How Renting Works

Quick booking, friendly in-person pickup. Here's exactly what to expect from start to finish.

The Process

Simple. Friendly. In Person.

Book online, then handle everything else face to face with our team at our Long Island City office — fast, clear, and fully guided.

01
📱

Reserve Online in Minutes

Choose your vehicle, enter your dates, and submit your driver's license. Booking is confirmed in minutes — no phone calls required ahead of time.

02
📍

Receive Your Pickup Instructions

After booking, you'll receive a confirmation with everything you need for pickup:

📍
Office Address

30-10 41st Ave, Long Island City, NY 11101. Head straight there at your scheduled pickup time.

🧾
What to Bring

Your valid driver's license and the card used to book. Our team will have your reservation ready to go.

03
✅

Check In & Get Your Keys

When you arrive at our office, our team verifies your identity and does a quick pre-rental walkthrough of the vehicle with you. Once that's done, we hand you the keys — you're ready to drive.

04
🚗

Drive — You're All Set

Enjoy your rental. If you have any questions during your rental period, our support team is available to assist you. Extensions can be requested via SMS before your scheduled return time.

05
🔁

Return the Vehicle

Drive back to our office, hand the keys to our team, and we'll do a quick final check together. That's it — fast, friendly, no hassle.

Good to Know

Common questions about pickup

🔍

What if I can't find the office?

Contact our team immediately by phone or text and we'll guide you in. We're located at 30-10 41st Ave, Long Island City, NY 11101.

🕐

What if I'm running late to pickup?

Just text us. We'll do our best to accommodate. If another renter is scheduled after you, we may need to adjust, but we always communicate.

📸

Is the car's condition documented?

Yes — our team does a quick walkthrough with you at pickup and documents the vehicle's condition before your trip starts.

⛽

What about fuel?

Vehicles are provided with fuel. Please return the vehicle with at least the same fuel level as when you received it.
