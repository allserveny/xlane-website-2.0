Simple Process

Renting takes less than
5 minutes — here's how

From reservation to ignition, everything happens on your phone. No paperwork. No waiting in line. No office visit required.

1

Choose Your Car & Zone

Browse our available vehicles and select the New Jersey pickup zone closest to you. Each car has an exact GPS pin you'll receive after booking.

2

Book & Pay Securely

Submit your valid NJ or out-of-state driver's license, select rental dates, and pay with a card. Booking is confirmed instantly by email and SMS.

3

Unlock with Your Phone

Walk to the vehicle at pickup time. Complete the check-in process to unlock the car. Keys are inside.

4

Return & Lock Up

Drive back to the pickup zone, leave the key inside the car, and text our team to confirm drop off. Done — no drop-off desk needed.
