Our Story

We built XLane Rentals because renting a car shouldn't be a headache.

Traditional rental companies make you drive to an airport or mall, wait in line, sign stacks of paper, and argue over inspection scratches. We thought: there's a better way.

XLane Rentals was founded in 2022 by a group of NJ residents who were tired of the rental runaround. Today we operate 40+ vehicles across 6 zones in New Jersey — all managed remotely, all accessible contactlessly.

Our vehicles live in the communities we serve. When you rent from XLane Rentals, you're picking up from a real neighborhood in NJ — not schlepping to a remote airport lot. We're where you are.

No chatbots. Our team is reachable by text, call, or email around the clock. If something goes wrong — or even if something just feels unclear — a real person answers within minutes.

We're a real New Jersey business — here's the proof

We know that a company without a physical storefront can feel unfamiliar. That's fair. Here's what makes XLane Rentals legitimate and trustworthy:

XLane Rentals is registered with the New Jersey Division of Revenue and Enterprise Services. Our vehicles are registered and maintained to NJ DMV standards. Each car undergoes safety inspections every 6 months.

Our contactless model isn't a workaround — it's the point. By eliminating expensive office leases and large parking lots, we pass those savings directly to renters. It's the same reason you trust Airbnb hosts over hotels for certain trips.
