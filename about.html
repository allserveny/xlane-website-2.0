Our Story

We built XLane Rentals because renting a car shouldn't be a headache.

Traditional rental companies make you drive to an airport or mall, wait in line, sign stacks of paper, and argue over inspection scratches. We thought: there's a better way.

We're a real New York business — here's the proof

We know that a smaller rental company can feel unfamiliar compared to the big airport chains. That's fair. Here's what makes XLane Rentals legitimate and trustworthy:

XLane Rentals is registered with New York State and operates out of our office at 30-10 41st Ave, Long Island City, NY 11101. Our vehicles are registered and maintained to New York State DMV standards. Each car undergoes a safety inspection once every year.

By keeping a single, efficient office instead of multiple expensive airport counters, we pass those savings directly to renters — without sacrificing the in-person service and support you get with a traditional rental.
